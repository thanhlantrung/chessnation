about us

Chess Nation is a Berlin-based charity which uses chess as a means to achieve social goals. We believe that chess, with its rich cultural heritage and broad international reach, is much more than just a game and has the power to contribute to society: by bringing people together, fostering social inclusion, and supporting personal development.

Chess Nation was founded in 2019 and aims to set up a range of projects tailored for different groups. In all our activities we seek to involve people we believe will benefit especially. Our pipeline projects include setting up a children’s club in Neukölln, supporting chess in Berlin’s prisons, and promoting the game in villages in Brandenburg. In addition we run Chess For Fun, a weekly get-together in Neukölln which focuses on the social rather than the competitive side of chess.

As a young organisation, we are grateful for any and all support to steer us through the opening and reach a promising middlegame. Please drop us a line or come along to Chess For Fun if you would like to find out more, join our organisation, help with one of our existing projects, or have an idea for a new project. Chess Nation aims to be welcoming and inclusive and is open to all ideas that support our mission.
